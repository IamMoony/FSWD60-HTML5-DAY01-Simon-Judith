The Grinch

The Grinch is depicted as a hairy, pot-bellied, pear-shaped, snub-nosed creature with a cat-like face and cynical personality.
In full-color adaptations, he is typically colored avocado green.
He has spent the past 53 years living in seclusion on a cliff, overlooking the town of Whoville.
